Socials

Work hard, play hard. Socials are an important part of debate tournaments and the society activities. Debaters often form quick and lasting friendships at society weekly practices, events, socials and tournaments. We may be biased, but in our experience debaters tend to be witty and interesting people! Debaters usually go out to socialize, discuss and relax together after debate practices. The society also organizes dinners, game nights, sauna evenings, debate or current affairs related TV or movie viewing nights for its members from all different backgrounds and degree programs to come together to talk and have fun. Tournament socials allow you to expand your social circles even further and make lasting connections with debaters from other countries.
