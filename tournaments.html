Tournaments

Debating tournaments take place all around the world, usually hosted by the debating societies/clubs/unions of local Universities. These competitions allow you to test your debating mettle against debaters from all over the world. Society members have the opportunity to attend tournaments that are aimed towards different debating skill levels from novice to advanced. Tournaments usually take place over a weekend, with days dedicated to debating and evening socials where you can get to know debaters from all over Europe.
