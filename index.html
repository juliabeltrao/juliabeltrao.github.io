What is ADS?

Aalto Debating Society (ADS) is an AYY and KY recognized organisation that seeks to promote persuasive presentation and argumentation skills among Aalto University students. The society also practices for and has participants compete in University Debating tournaments.

We want to engage students in discussing topical issues and thinking critically. We organize workshops and major speaker events to help students present their ideas confidently and persuasively anytime, anywhere and in front of any audience.

Why should you consider joining ADS?

  • Weekly debate practices every Thursday at KY House 18:30, in Töölö!
  • Crazy debating tournaments all around Europe!
  • Workshops, major speaker events, and socials.
  • A vibrant social community with interesting people from all across Aalto, with various disciplines and from all around the world.

Who can join?

The society is open to any interested debate enthusiasts. If you have an interest in public speaking, a desire to argue, or even just want to brush up on your English proficiency ADS is a society at Aalto University worth checking out. Participants at our debates are not always Aalto University students, but high schoolers, students at other learning institutions or just the otherwise debate interested. ADS looks to develop debating culture and competency in Finland, which is why we cooperate with coaching Finnish High Schoolers and Finnish High School debating tournaments.
