Helsinki Open

Together with the debating society of Helsinki University (Helsinki Debating Society), ADS organizes the annual debating tournament Helsinki Open every Spring. Helsinki Open is an international tournament, which is open to all participants. The tournament has attracted debaters from all over Finland, Europe and as far away as the United States. The tournament is the largest international university debating tournament in Finland, attracting top-level adjudicators ensuring the quality of the tournaments motions and proceedings. We have proudly cooperated with top-notch companies, such as Boston Consulting Group and Stora Enso, which are happy to encourage debating, as it teaches many of the skills they look for in their future employees.

Check out the most recent or upcoming Helsinki Open Debating Tournament website at helsinkiopen.org
