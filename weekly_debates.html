Weekly debates

Everyone is welcome to visit our weekly practices and learn what debate is all about!

Thursdays at 18:30, KY House in Töölö, Helsinki. You can find more information about our weekly debates on our Facebook page.

Debating is a great way to improve your speaking and argumentation skills and to learn about the international format for university debates, British Parliamentary debating. Our practices encourage participants to hone their skills through practice and constructive feedback from more experienced debaters.
